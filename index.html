Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions you'd like answered please feel free to email us.

What is Bookmark?
Bookmark is a tool that helps you organize and manage your favorite websites in one place.
How can I request a new browser?
You can request a new browser by contacting us via email or through our support page.
Is there a mobile app?
Currently, we do not have a mobile app, but it is something we are considering for the future.
What about other Chromium browsers?
Our extension works on most Chromium-based browsers, including Edge and Brave.
